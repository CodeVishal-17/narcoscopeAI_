Pipeline
From a public post to an evidence-backed lead — four stages, a human in the loop before any of it leaves the system.

Detection engine
Built to catch how trafficking actually shows up on each platform — not a generic keyword filter.

Telegram bots & channels

Detects /menu-style command bots, price-list patterns, and "backup channel" links posted when a storefront gets banned and reappears under a new name.

Instagram handles & stories

Reads coded bios and caption patterns ("DM for price," emoji substitution) that evade plain keyword filters.

Cross-platform correlation

Same UPI ID or payment handle showing up on a Telegram channel and an Instagram profile is treated as one operator, not two separate leads.

Scope & limits
What this system does — and deliberately does not do.
IP addresses, phone numbers, and email IDs are not extracted from public content — platforms don't expose that data, and no legitimate tool can. NarcoScope AI's job ends at building a well-evidenced dossier; obtaining subscriber records requires an investigator to submit that evidence to the platform through lawful process — Section 91 CrPC/BNSS, or an MLAT request for foreign platforms. Every escalation from this system passes through human analyst review before it goes anywhere.
